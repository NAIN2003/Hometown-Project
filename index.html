Top three activities to do at MULTAN

The Multan Fort

The Multan Fort was a historic fort in the city of Multan. According to some estimates the original fort was built by Katoch Dynasty Rajput between 800 and 1000 B.C.

Garrison Public Library Multan

Garrison Public Library Multan (GPL Multan) has been established under the auspices of Pakistan Army.

Multan Cricket Stadium

The Multan Cricket Stadium is a multi-purpose stadium in Multan, Punjab, Pakistan. The stadium is located off Vehari Road, in the suburbs of Multan. It has a capacity of 35,000.
